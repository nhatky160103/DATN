## Datasets

### Training Datasets

22. **CASIA-WebFace Database**  
    Dong Yi, Zhen Lei, Shengcai Liao, Stan Z. Li  
    *arXiv preprint*, 2014  
    [arXiv:1411.7923](https://arxiv.org/abs/1411.7923)  
    (~500K images, 10K identities)

23. **MS-Celeb-1M: A Dataset and Benchmark for Large-Scale Face Recognition**  
    Yandong Guo, Lei Zhang, Yuxiao Hu, Xiaodong He, Jianfeng Gao  
    *European Conference on Computer Vision (ECCV), 2016*  
    (Refined version: MS1MV3 with ~93K identities, ~5.2M images)

24. **VGGFace2: A Dataset for Recognising Faces across Pose and Age**  
    Qiong Cao, Li Shen, Weidi Xie, Omkar M. Parkhi, Andrew Zisserman  
    *IEEE International Conference on Automatic Face & Gesture Recognition (FG), 2018*  
    [arXiv:1710.08092](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.08092)

25. **Labeled Faces in the Wild (LFW): A Database for Studying Face Recognition in Unconstrained Environments**  
    Gary B. Huang, Manu Ramesh, Tamara Berg, Erik Learned-Miller  
    *University of Massachusetts Technical Report*, 2007  

26. **The MegaFace Benchmark: 1 Million Faces for Recognition at Scale**  
    Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman, Steven M. Seitz, Daniel Miller, Evan Brossard  
    *Proceedings of the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2016*

27. **IARPA Janus Benchmark-B (IJB-B) Face Dataset**  
    Brianna Maze, Jocelyn Adams, James A. Duncan, et al.  
    *IEEE International Conference on Biometrics: Theory, Applications and Systems (BTAS), 2018*

28. **IARPA Janus Benchmark-C (IJB-C)**
